## 🚀 Getting Started

Launch the application using the new desktop shortcut. When you open the program for the first time, you must create an account using your email address. The application uses this account to manage your image credits and secure your data.

### Uploading Your Product
To start a project, click the "New Project" button. You will see a prompt to upload images of your product. For the best AI results, use clear photos shot against a neutral background. High-resolution images allow the AI to detect product edges and details with accuracy.

### Using Templates
The software includes a library of preset templates. These templates define the background, lighting, and composition of your final image. Browse the categories to find a scene that matches your brand style. After selecting a template, the AI analyzes your uploaded image and merges it into the chosen scene.

### Generating Images
After you select a template, click the "Generate" button. The software sends your request to the image generation engine. This process takes a few seconds. You can track the progress using the indicator on your screen. Once the task finishes, your result appears in your project dashboard. 

## ⚙️ Managing Settings

The settings menu allows you to adjust how the system processes your photos. You can change your default image export format, such as JPEG or PNG. You can also monitor your remaining credits in the "Billing" section. If you need more credits to generate additional images, use the integrated payment portal to upgrade your plan.

## 🛠 Troubleshooting

If you encounter issues during installation or usage, check these common items:

*   **Application does not launch**: Restart your computer and try again. Ensure no other heavy software is consuming all your system memory.
*   **Failed image generation**: Check your internet connection. AI generation requires a server-side handshake, which needs an active connection.
*   **Poor image quality**: Ensure your source photos contain good light and sharp focus. The AI performs better with clear input data.
*   **Missing installation file**: If your browser blocks the download, check your security software settings to confirm it allows files from the distribution link.
